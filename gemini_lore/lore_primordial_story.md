# Primordial

this is the game. please welcome.

the aim of this game is crime, and lots of it.

but relax. you are not starting with bank jobs. you are starting as a grown baby (toddler) in a house with rules, and the first rule is the stupidest one:

you are meant to be asleep.

you begin in your bedroom. the lights are off. the house is doing its night routine. pipes tick. the fridge hums. the tv in the other room dies slowly. adult footsteps fade like weather.

bedtime is a soft law. the kind you can test without anyone calling you a monster.

and you test it.

you do not move. you just stay awake. you let the minutes stack up. you listen for the moment the house stops looking for you.

nothing explodes.

this is the primordial lesson: rules are not walls. rules are a cost. if you can pay it, you can do the thing anyway.

you can quit right here.

close the game. go outside. touch some blades of grass. drink water. be a person with a future. no judgement. (some judgement.)

or you can keep going.

the house is a system. during the day it belongs to the adults. at night it belongs to whoever is awake enough to use it.

you need a tool. you take a torch. you take it quietly. you take it like it does not count.

under the covers you read a boring book with stolen light and suddenly it tastes different. you are not just reading. you are getting away with something.

then the brain does the thing it always does when a rule breaks cleanly.

it offers you another one.

your dad is a cop. he brings work home like stink. there is a room you are not meant to be in. there is a heavy book that is not for you. it is a manual for catching people like the future version of you.

you borrow it.

borrow is the word your brain uses when it wants to sleep at night. steal is the word the house would use, if the house could talk.

you read about evidence. you read about habits. you read about how people get found, and the secret is disappointing:

they get found because they get sloppy.

now you have the enemy team's map in your head. now you understand the game is not good vs evil.

it is method vs laziness.

take your time. commit a crime.

when you find a crime you can press `D` to sort out the details first, or press `Q` to just go for it. both are valid. one is embarrassing.

then you wait. this game is about waiting, but hopefully not too long.

after waiting, you get success or not.

use success to access more crime.

use more crime to access more waiting.
